# gameplay 
1. a random letter appears in front of you . for a sprcific time (it decreases as the level progress)
2. Then you choose the correct answer from tho options
3. After level 5 . the number of choices increases from 2 to 6 and stop in level 10 . And the time is fixed at 0.5.
4. last . in level 10 to 15 (the final) you only have 2.5s to choose the correct letter .
5. if you finish level 15 you win the game.
